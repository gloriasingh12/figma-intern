# Logo Design & Branding Mockups
**Project:** Brand Identity for "TechAditya" / "Salaam Namaste Tech"
**Deliverable:** 3 Conceptual Variations with Design Logic.

---

## 🎨 Variation 1: The Modern Minimalist (Geometric)
* **Concept:** Using sharp edges and clean lines to represent precision and coding.
* **Visual:** A stylized 'A' & 'T' integrated into a single geometric shape (Hexagon or Diamond).
* **Color Palette:** Deep Blue (#003366) and Electric Cyan.
* **Logic:** Represents "Modernity" and "Scalability" in Cloud computing.

## 🎨 Variation 2: The Community Connector (Organic)
* **Concept:** Focused on the "Radio" and "Human Connection" aspect of Salaam Namaste.
* **Visual:** A microphone icon that subtly blends into a Wi-Fi or Signal wave.
* **Color Palette:** Warm Orange (#FF8C00) and Charcoal Grey.
* **Logic:** Represents "Approachability" and "Voice" in the community.

## 🎨 Variation 3: The Cyber-Security Shield (Abstract)
* **Concept:** Focusing on the 'DevSecOps' and 'Security' tasks performed.
* **Visual:** A shield outline with a circuit-board pattern inside it.
* **Color Palette:** Emerald Green (#50C878) and Midnight Black.
* **Logic:** Represents "Protection", "Integrity", and "Advanced Technology".

---

## 🛠️ Design Process (Figma Workflow)
1. **Wireframing:** Sketching initial concepts on paper.
2. **Vectorization:** Using Figma's 'Pen Tool' to create clean paths.
3. **Typography:** Selecting Sans-Serif fonts (like Montserrat or Roboto) for a tech feel.
4. **Mockup:** Placing the logo on a Smartphone and Business Card mockup.
